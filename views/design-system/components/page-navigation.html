Optional Icons

The FCDO page navigation may optionally include icons alongside navigation labels to provide additional visual cues for users.

Icons can help users quickly identify the purpose of different sections, particularly on content-heavy pages, and can improve scannability and accessibility when used consistently.Where icons are included, they should support the meaning of the label rather than replace it, remain simple and recognisable
